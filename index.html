REACT & ACT!
The rules
One player is given a scenario that they have to react to, while the other players try to guess what the scenario is. The player has to use his emotions and body to act out the scenario, no speaking allowed. If a player is able to guess the scenario before the time rouns out, the acting player gets 1 point and the player that guessed correctly gets 2 points. If no one is able to guess it, no points are given.
